You must also assign a confidence score between 0 and 1, 1 being absolute certainty, to each cluster you make. Please respond only with first a list of lists of indices indicating the clusters, for example: [[0,1,4], [2, 3]] indicates two clusters, the first contains the 0th, 1st, and 4th entities in the users list.
